New York State Car Registrations by County

30 JAN 2019 • 1 min read

Dash makes it easy to build interactive web apps to analyze and visualize data, and the state of New York provides data on all vehicle registrations. Combining the two, I built an app and deployed it using Heroku. Here’s a screenshot using the brand Subaru.

The dataset includes all vehicle registrations over the last 2 years, and I filtered the data to cars with a model year of 2016 or later in an attempt to capture new car sales. Subaru is interesting because we see many contiguous counties with greater than 10% of registrations. Open the app and explore other brands!
